Connection lines

🔒 Encrypted (HTTPS) — content hidden in transit
🛡️ VPN tunnel — even the destination is hidden
🔓 Not encrypted (HTTP) — anyone in between can read it

HTTP vs HTTPS

🔓 HTTP

The ISP and VPN can read the full page and anything you type — passwords included.

🔒 HTTPS

Content is encrypted. They still see which site you visit and when — just not what you do there.

All IP addresses are fake, from the ranges reserved for teaching (RFC 5737).
A VPN hides your IP — but cookies and logins can still identify you.
